## Claim

Microsoft's GraphRAG is an inference technique that builds temporary knowledge graphs on-the-fly from unstructured text to answer queries. It does not solve persistent, curated graph storage. It could be used to auto-populate the ike graph from documents, but the persistent YAML-based graph is still needed as the canonical, maintained source of truth.
